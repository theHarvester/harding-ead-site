Services

Do you need quality electrical work with an affordable price tag? Harding Electrical & Data service the Greater Brisbane region for all of your residential, commercial & industrial needs.

Residential

No job is too big or small. Covering all of your electrical residential needs from powerpoints, lighting & fans right through to house wiring/rewiring & renovations. We’re big on energy efficiency to save you money.

Commercial

Let us take care of your installations, maintenance & repairs so that you can get on with running your business. We’ve got all of your cabling, lighting, testing & tagging needs covered. Plus we offer maintenance 24/7.

Industrial

We’re here to help with your industrial lighting, switchboard & power needs. Plus we can take care of any breakdowns or maintenance required and offer 24/7 service. We’ll also monitor electricity to save you money.

What we can do for you

We service the Greater Brisbane area providing a full range of electrical services for industrial, commercial & residential. We use the most energy efficient products & equipment to save you & the environment.

There’s no job that’s too big or small, from replacing a power point, fault finding, safety switch tests to larger jobs such as renovations, house rewiring & wiring. We’re also actively involved in our local community and recently pitched in to help the Johnson family with Brett’s Backyard Rescue.
